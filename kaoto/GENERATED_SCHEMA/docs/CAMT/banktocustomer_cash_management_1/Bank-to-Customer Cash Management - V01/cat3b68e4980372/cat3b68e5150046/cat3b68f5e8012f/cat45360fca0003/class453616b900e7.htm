<<Message>> BankToCustomerDebitCreditNotificationV01 {Analysis}

Documentation
The Bank-to-Customer Debit Credit Notification message is sent by the
account servicer to an account owner or to a party authorised by the account
owner to receive the message. It can be used to inform the account owner,
or authorised party, of single or multiple debit and or credit entries reported
to the account.
