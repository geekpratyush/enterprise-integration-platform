<<Amount>> EuroMax15Amount {Analysis}

Documentation
Number of monetary units specified in a currency, where the unit of currency is the EUR and compliant with ISO 4217. The decimal separator is a dot, of maximum 15 digits before the dot and 2 after.
Note: A zero amount is not allowed
