If PaymentMethodByFirstAgent is present and is CHK, then PaymentTransaction/ChequeInstruction is optional.
If PaymentMethodByFirstAgent is present and is not CHK, then PaymentTransaction/ChequeInstruction is not allowed.
If PaymentMethodByFirstAgent is not present, then PaymentTransaction/ChequeInstruction is not allowed.

Rule rationale: ChequeInstructionDetails may be present if the payment method is Cheque. It must not be present if the payment method is 'Transfer'.
