Documentation
Occurs when the instructing party requests to cancel a previously instructed payment.

This case is initially assigned by an instructing party to an instructed party. The assigned case can be forwarded through the payment chain.

The potential outcome of such a case is:

- the cancellation request is rejected. This happens if the creditor refuses to reimburse the funds.
- the cancellation is accepted. The effective return of funds needs to be handled through specific instructions.
