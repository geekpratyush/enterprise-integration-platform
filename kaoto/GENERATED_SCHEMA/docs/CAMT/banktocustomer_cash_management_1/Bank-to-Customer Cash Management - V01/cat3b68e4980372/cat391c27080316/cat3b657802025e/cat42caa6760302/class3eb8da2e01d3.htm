Trace To Item DefinitionProvides the details for negotiating and trading a large number of securities contained in or comprising a portfolio. One example is index arbitrage, which consists in the purchase or sale of a basket of stocks in conjunction with the sale or purchase of a derivative product (for example index futures) to profit from price differences between the basket and the derivative product. Other examples include liquidation of EFP (Exchange for Physical) stock positions, portfolio realignment and portfolio liquidation.Examples
