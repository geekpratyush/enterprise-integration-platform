<<Code>> TradeExecutionSessionCode {Analysis}

Documentation
Represents a specific market trading session. It is used to identify a portion of the session where the execution might take place ("after market", "pre-market").
It is used to support order routing, quoting, and trade and market data reporting. For instance, orders can be routed to one or more Trading Sessions. Examples of Trading Session usage may be to facilitate extended hours trading, to differentiate between concurrent electronic and open-outcry trading, or to differentiate block and/or oddlot trading.
It is used by Exchanges, ECN's, and ATS's.
