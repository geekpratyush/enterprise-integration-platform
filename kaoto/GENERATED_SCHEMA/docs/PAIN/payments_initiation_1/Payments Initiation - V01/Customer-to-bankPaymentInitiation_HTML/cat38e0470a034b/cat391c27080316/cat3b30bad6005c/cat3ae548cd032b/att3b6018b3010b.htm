String Cheque = CHEC, in Class CashSettlementSystem1Code

Documentation
A written order from one party (the drawer) to another (the drawee, normally a financial institution) requiring the drawee to pay a specified sum on demand to the drawer or to a third party specified by the drawer. Cheques may be used for settling debts and withdrawing money from financial institutions.
