Documentation
Occurs when insufficient or incorrect information prevents processing of a payment instruction (eg account number missing or incorrect, account closed, name and account do not match, missing final agent, etc.). Processing of a payment covers both the execution of the instruction and the reconciliation of the instruction.

This case is initially assigned by an instructed party to an instructing party. The assigned case can be forwarded back through the payment chain.


The potential solutions to such a case are:

- the missing information is provided
- the incorrect information is modified
- the instruction is cancelled, if it happens that the instruction is not actually related to the payment due.
