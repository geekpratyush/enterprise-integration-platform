Documentation
Occurs when a party is expecting a payment and is unable to retrieve this payment.

The party expecting the payment contacts the party which initiated the payment. This party is effectively initiating the case. The assigned case can be forwarded through the payment chain.

The potential reasons for such a case are:

- the payment is pending somewhere. Investigations need to be handled to identify the reason why it is pending. It might result in a modification or a cancellation of an instruction.

- the payment has been made to another party. It may result in a cancellation of this payment and in a new instruction.

- the payment has been made to the right party, but some information is missing or incorrect to allow this party to reconcile the payment.

Note: this case also covers the "missing cover" exception.
