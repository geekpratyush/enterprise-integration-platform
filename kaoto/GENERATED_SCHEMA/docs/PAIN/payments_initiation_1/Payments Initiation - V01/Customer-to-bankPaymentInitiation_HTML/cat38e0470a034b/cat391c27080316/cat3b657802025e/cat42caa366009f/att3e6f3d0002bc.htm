Documentation
Central Securities Depository that operates in a particular domestic market for specified financial instruments. It holds or controls the holding of physical or dematerialised financial instruments belonging to all or a large portion of the investors in a securities market, and which effects the centralised transfer of such securities against payment (or free of payment) by entries on its books and records, resulting in transfer and record of ownership of the securities.
