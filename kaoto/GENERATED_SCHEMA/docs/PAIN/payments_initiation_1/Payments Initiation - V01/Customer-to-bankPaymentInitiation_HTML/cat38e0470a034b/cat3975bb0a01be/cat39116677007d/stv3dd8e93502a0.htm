Activity Forward debit advices

Parent State/ActivityNone  


Documentation
The forwarding agent forwards to the initiating party a notification received from the first agent that the debit entry has been passed on the account of the debtor/initiating party.

This debit advice will be further used by the initiating party for the reconciliation of entries vis-à-vis payment orders received/remitted.
