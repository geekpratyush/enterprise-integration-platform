<<Actor>> DebitParty {Analysis}

Documentation
The party that gives cash. In the payment chain, it may be an agent or the debtor. Furthermore, for each pair of consecutive parties in the payment chain, one party always plays the role of the debit party, and the other, the role of the credit party.
