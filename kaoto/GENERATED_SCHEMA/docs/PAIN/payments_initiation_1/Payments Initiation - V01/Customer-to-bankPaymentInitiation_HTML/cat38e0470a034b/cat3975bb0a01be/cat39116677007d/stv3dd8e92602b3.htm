Documentation
The initiating party receives a notification from the first agent, (possibly through the forwarding agent - if any), that the debit entry has been passed on the account of the debtor (which may or may not be equal to the initiating party).

This debit advice will be further used for the reconciliation of entries vis-à-vis payment orders received/remitted.
