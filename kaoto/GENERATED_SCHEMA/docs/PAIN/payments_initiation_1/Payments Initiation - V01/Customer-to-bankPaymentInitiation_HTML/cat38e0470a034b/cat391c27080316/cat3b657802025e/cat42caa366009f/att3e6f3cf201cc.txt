Holds or controls the holding of physical or dematerialised financial instruments belonging to all or a  large portion of the investors in a securities market, and which effects the centralised transfer of such securities against payment (or free of payment) by entries on its books and records, resulting in transfer and record of ownership of the securities.  A distinction can be made between a Central Securities Depository (CSD), which operates in a particular domestic market for specified financial instruments or an International Central Securities Depository (ICSD), ie, Euroclear and Clearstream, which settle Eurobond instruments and have direct or indirect links with specific CSDs.
