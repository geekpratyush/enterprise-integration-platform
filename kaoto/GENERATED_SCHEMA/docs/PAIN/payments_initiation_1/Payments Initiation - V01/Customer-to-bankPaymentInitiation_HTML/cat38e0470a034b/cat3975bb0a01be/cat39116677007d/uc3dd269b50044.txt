The first agent has to issue certified and non certified cheques. 
It has been decided not to further define 'certified' and 'uncertified' cheques, as practices vary widely in different regions/countries. The process of issuing cheques will be further detailed in the activity diagram, insofar as it differs from executing an ordinary credit transfer order.

Principles that seem to be common in Europe :
For certified cheque, the initiating party is debited before the cheque is issued. 

For non-certified cheque, the initiating party is only debited after cashing of the cheque by the final party.
