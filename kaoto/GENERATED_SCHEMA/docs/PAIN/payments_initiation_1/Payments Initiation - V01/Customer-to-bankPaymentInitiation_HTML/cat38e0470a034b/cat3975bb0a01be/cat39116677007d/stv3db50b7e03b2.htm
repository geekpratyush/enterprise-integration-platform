Activity Prepare payment orders

Parent State/ActivityNone  


Documentation
An initiating party collects the information necessary to initiate a payment to the forwarding or first agent.

Depending whether the initiating party is a corporate or an individual, the business information needed in the business process is slightly different.

Note : we have added here the arguments necessary to prepare the payment order, both from a corporate and a private consumer point of view.

Information to be submitted by the Corporate to the forwarding or first agent :

· debtor/creditor's account/bank, debtor/creditor's name - debtor id : customer ID (may be several ID)
· contact details
· (total) payment amount,requested execution date and/or required due date (for beneficiary's.bank)
· remittance information and/or agreement parties references related to the agreement and/or creditor's references related to the payment, and/or debtor' s references related to the payment : unique ID for payment order, batch reference,
· direction indicator (debit or credit)
· charges bearer (creditor, debtor or shared)
· exchange currency
· FX contract reference and/or pre-agreed rate
· payment method (how should the beneficiary be paid) (eg electronic transfer, cheque, specific service )/payment instrument/system
· cheque back to originator indicator (information for first agent - issuing cheque : does not apply to 'prepare payment order' - but to 'prepare cheque ordering' in cheque activity diagram)
· priority flag : values normal/urgent (boolean value : urgent or not)
· payment reason/purpose :(for central bank reporting/for statement purposes - for information for the beneficiary customer)
· country of residence of ordinating.customer/beneficiary customer.
· Information on agents
· Indicator : single or batch debit entry

Information to be submitted by the individual consumer to the forwarding or first agent :

All of the above applies except :
· contact details
· indicator : single or batch debit entry (as consumer could transmit payments as batch but will always want individual entries)
