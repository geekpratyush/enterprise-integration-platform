<<BusinessComponent>> InvestigationCase {Analysis}

Documentation
Represents a set of activities performed to handle an exception to a normal transaction flow.

Payment investigation cases have several root causes, eg:

- a payment has not been received
- a payment has been received but is incorrect.

In addition, the party which ordered the payment could request its cancellation or its modification.

Such request for cancellation or modification could also be the result of a payment not received or incorrect.

This leads to the four defined specialisations:

UnableToApplyCase
ClaimNonReceiptCase
RequestToCancelCase
RequestToModifyCase
