<<business use case>> Order customer-to-bank payment

Documentation
Definition : an initiating party orders to its financial institution a payment related and possibly referring to the underlying business transactions (eg invoices).

Pre-condition : the required (identifying) information is available to make a payment.

Trigger : decision has been made to make a payment (either by a person or a system).

Post-condition : the payment is delivered by the initiating party to the forwarding or first agent.
