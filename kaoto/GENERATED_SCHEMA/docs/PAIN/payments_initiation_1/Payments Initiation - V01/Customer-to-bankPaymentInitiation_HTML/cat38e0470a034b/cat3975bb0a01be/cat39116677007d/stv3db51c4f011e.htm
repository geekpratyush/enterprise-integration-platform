Activity Prepare debit booking confirmations

Parent State/ActivityNone  


Documentation
The first agent prepares the information to send to the initiating party or forwarding agent (relay scenario) explaining that the payment is executed i.e. that the account is debited.

Pre-condition : the payment is settled.

Trigger : the account has been debited.

Post-condition : the initiating party may be informed that the payment is settled and may start the payment reconcilation and tracking.
