<<business use case>> Accept customer-to-bank payment

Documentation
The payment acceptation includes the checking of the authorisation, the validation of the payment and the payment risk assessment.

Pre-condition : the first or forwarding agent has received the payment order.

Trigger : receipt of the payment order.

Post condition : the payment has been accepted or rejected.
