<<BusinessProcess>> Authorize

Documentation
Definition : the authorization of a payment order by the initiating party. This is a subprocess of the 'order payment' process.

An authorization may be implicit if the system where the payment is generated has been approved to generate payments, as the preceding procedures are deemed satisfactory secure. The system will then generate a digital signature without manual intervention.
An authorization may be explicit, if procedures require human approval. The representation of this approval will be included in the payment order or sent to the forwarding or first agent as a separate message.

Pre-condition : the order payment process has completed and waits for authorization.

Trigger : a payment order has been created.

Post-condition : the payment order is authorized.
