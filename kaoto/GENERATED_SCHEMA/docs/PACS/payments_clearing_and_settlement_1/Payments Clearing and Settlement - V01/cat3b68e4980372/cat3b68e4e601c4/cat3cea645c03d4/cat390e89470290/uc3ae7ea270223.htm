<<BusinessProcess>> Process interbank payment

Documentation
Definition : perform the cash transfer and proceed the transfer of cash ownership from a party to the next party in the payment chain, if needed.

Pre-condition : the clearing agent knows the previous party.

Trigger : the payment has been remitted.

Post-condition : transfer of ownership of cash has been performed between a debit party and credit party. When the credit party is the creditor, the payment process is completed.
