<<BusinessProcess>> Process E2E payment transaction

Documentation
Definition : an initiating party orders to its financial institution a payment related and possible referring to the underlying business transactions (eg invoices).

The payment will result in cash transfers between debtors and creditors through clearing agents and settlement agents.

Pre-condition : there is an agreement (explicit or implicit in case of gift) that requests a cash transfer between an initiating party (which may or may not be equal to the debtor) and a final party (which may or may not be equal to the creditor).The agreement (purchase and/or payment) may include conditions (purchase and/or payment) and/or assurance terms (purchase and/or payment).

Trigger: decision has been made to make a payment.

Post-condition : the payment transaction is settled ie the final party has received the cash.
