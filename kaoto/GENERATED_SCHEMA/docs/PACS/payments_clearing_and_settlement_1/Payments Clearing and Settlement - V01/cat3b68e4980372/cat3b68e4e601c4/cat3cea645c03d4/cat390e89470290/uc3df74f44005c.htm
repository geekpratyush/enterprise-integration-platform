<<BusinessProcess>> Settle payment

Documentation
Definition :the first agent agent performs the transfer of cash between the debit party (ie the debtor) and the credit party (ie the first agent itself).
The final agent agent performs the transfer of cash between the debit party (ie the final agent itself) and the credit party (ie the creditor).


Pre-condition : instructed payment is executable (all information is there to process the payment, the payment validity has been checked).

Trigger : payment execution date in the payment is reached

Post-condition : transfer of ownership is performed between the debit party and credit party.
