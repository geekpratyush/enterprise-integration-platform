<<BusinessProcess>> Generate regulatory reporting information

Documentation

Definition:the forwarding or first agent selects the information from the payment transaction necessary to meet regulatory reporting requirements (eg balance of payments reporting, reporting on money-laundering issues,)


Pre-condition:there are regulatory reporting requirements.

Trigger:the payment meets the regulatory reporting criteria.

Post-condition:the information for the regulatory reporting has been extracted based on information in the payment.
