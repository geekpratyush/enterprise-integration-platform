<<BusinessProcess>> Check authorization payment

Documentation
Definition:the forwarding (if any) or first agent whether the party/parties sending and authorizing the payment :
· are conform to the parties allowed to do so as included in the authorization specifications of the customer profile at the forwarding or first agent.
· are entitled to send and authorize the type of payments they are sending/authorizing as included in the authorization specifications of the customer profile at the forwarding or first agent
· respect and apply the authorization procedures as agreed in the authorization specifications of the customer profile at the forwarding or first agent.

Note : Authorisation procedures are in some countries required for legal reasons.
The result of these authorization procedures can, depending on the authorisation scenario defined, be included in the payment initiation order (eg one or more signatures included in the payment initiation order), or can result in a dissociated authorization message, sent by another party than the initiating party, which is referring to the payment initiation message.


Pre-condition: the forwarding /first agent and initiating party have established authorisation procedures for sending and receiving payments.

Trigger:payment has been sent by the initiating party and has arrived at the forwarding (if any) or first agent.

Post-condition:the forwarding/first agent will either reject or accept the payment for further acceptance steps such as validation and - when applicable- risk assessment.
