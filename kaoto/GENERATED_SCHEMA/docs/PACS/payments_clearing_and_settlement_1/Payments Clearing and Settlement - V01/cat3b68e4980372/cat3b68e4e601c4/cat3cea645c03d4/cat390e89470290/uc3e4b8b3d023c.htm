<<BusinessProcess>> Clear interbank payment

Documentation
Definition : preparation of the payment information by the payment clearing agent for the next clearing agent in the interbank chain.
This process of preparing payment information can be divided into the sub-processes 'Sort payment' and 'prepare onward interbank payment' .


Pre-condition : the payment is executable.

Trigger : payment execution date is reached.

Post-condition : the onward interbankpayment is prepared
