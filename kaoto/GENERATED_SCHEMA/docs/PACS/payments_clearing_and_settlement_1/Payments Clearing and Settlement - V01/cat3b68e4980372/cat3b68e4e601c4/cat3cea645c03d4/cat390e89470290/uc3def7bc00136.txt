Definition : the clearing agent collects the information necessary to remit the payment to the next party (clearing and/or settlement agent). Then the clearing agent remits the payment to the party.

Arguments :

In case of a bulk payment, some information might be meaningful either for the bulk payment (for example, total payment) or for each individual payment or both to realise the process. Each information is marked to indicate the level of applicability.

-debtor/creditor's account/bank, creditor/debtor's name (individual payment)
-debit/credit party's account/bank, credit/debit party's name (bulk payment)
-(total) payment amount, execution date and/or value date (bulk payment- individual payment)
-remittance information and/or agreement parties references related to the agreement and/or creditor's references related to the payment, and/or debtor' s references related to the payment (individual payment)
-credit/debit party's references (bulk payment)
-bank operation code (bulk payment)
-direction indicator (debit or credit) (bulk payment - individual payment)
-charges bearer (creditor, debtor or shared) (either bulk payment if there is condition specific to the bulk otherwise individual payment) 
-exchange currency (individual payment)
-FX contract reference and/or pre-agreed rate  (either bulk payment if there is condition specific to the bulk otherwise individual payment) 
-settlement agent(s) (bulk payment)

Pre-condition : the payment is accepted, the next credit party is not the creditor and the next clearing agent is determined

Trigger : the cash has been received 

Post-condition : the onward payment is ready
