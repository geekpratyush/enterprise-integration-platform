<<Actor_S>> LocalMarketIntermediary {Analysis}

Documentation
An intermediary in the proxy voting chain that is active in the same market as where the shares are safekept. Collects and receives meeting information from the initiator and/or the proxy information provider and forwards it to the facilitator and/or investor. Collects voting instructions from the faciliatator and/or investor and forwards them to the initiator. May verify the entitlement.
