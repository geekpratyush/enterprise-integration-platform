Specifies the price at which the trade has been executed.
In the case where several transactions have been necessary to fulfil the trade order, then the executed trade price could be an average price.
If there is only one trade transaction for the execution of the trade, then the executed trade price could equal the deal price (unless, for example, the price includes commissions or rounding, or some other factor has been applied to the deal price or the executed trade price, or both).
