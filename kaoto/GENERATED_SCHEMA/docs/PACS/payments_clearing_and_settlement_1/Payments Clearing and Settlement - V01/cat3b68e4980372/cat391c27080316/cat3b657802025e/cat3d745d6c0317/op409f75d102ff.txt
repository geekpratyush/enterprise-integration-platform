If PaymentMethodBy FirstAgent is CHK and if PaymentTransaction/ChequeInstruction/DeliveryMethod is present and is MLFA, CRFA, RGFA or PUFA, then PaymentTransaction/FinalAgent is mandatory.
If PaymentMethodBy FirstAgent is CHK and if PaymentTransaction/ChequeInstruction/DeliveryMethod is not present or is not MLFA, CRFA, RGFA or PUFA, then PaymentTransaction/FinalAgent is not allowed.
