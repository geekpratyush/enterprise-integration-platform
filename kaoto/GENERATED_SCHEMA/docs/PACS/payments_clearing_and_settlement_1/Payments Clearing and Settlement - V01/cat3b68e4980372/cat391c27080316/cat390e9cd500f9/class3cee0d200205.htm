BusinessComment.0.TextEither the PaymentAgreement defines one amount to be transferred from one debtor to one creditor along one payment chain, in which case there will be one payment transaction. Or the amount is split into many sub-amounts with the possible cases: -the sub-amounts will be transferred from one debtor to one creditor through many payment chains -the sub-amounts will be transferred from one debtor to many creditors, each pair (creditor, debtor) having one payment chain -the sub-amounts will be transferred from many debtors to one creditor, each pair (creditor, debtor) having one payment chainBusinessComment.0.TypeIf the nondelivery indicator = "true", there must be a settlement method.
