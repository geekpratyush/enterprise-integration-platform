Payments & Clearing

The transition from legacy SWIFT MT (Message Type) formats to the ISO 20022 MX (XML) standard is one of the most significant overhauls in global financial messaging. MX messages are data-rich, meaning MX-to-MT (down-translation) results in data truncation, while MT-to-MX (up-translation) requires padding mandatory ISO fields.
